About

My Name is Rigelle

I'm a full-stack developer who loves technology and enjoys figuring out how things work. I'm always up for challenges and find learning and solving problems exciting.

I work well in teams and can also handle projects on my own. Besides my technical abilities, I find joy in expressing my creativity through art, animation, film, and design.
